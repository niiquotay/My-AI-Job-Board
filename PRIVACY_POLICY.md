# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: February 2026**

At CaliberDesk, your privacy is our priority. This policy outlines how we handle your data.

## 1. Information Collection
- **Account Data**: Name, email, and authentication metadata from Google/LinkedIn.
- **Professional Data**: CVs, work history, skills, and video pitches.
- **Technical Data**: IP addresses, browser types, and usage telemetry.

## 2. Use of Information
- To match candidates with relevant job opportunities using AI.
- To facilitate communication between employers and seekers.
- To improve our Neural Intelligence matching algorithms.

## 3. Data Sharing
- Your profile is visible to employers whose jobs you apply for.
- We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

## 4. Security
- We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) and secure storage protocols via Supabase.

## 5. Your Rights
- You can delete your account and all associated data at any time via the System Config menu.
